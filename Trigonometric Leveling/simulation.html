Trigonometric Leveling

Objective:

To find the elevation of structure when base is accessible.



STEP  1 
 Place the theodolite at point a.  

STEP  2 
  Click on level theodolite to level it. Then use the controls to adjust bubble to the centre of bubble tube  













STEP  3 
 Click on "Angle Measure" to measure the angle of top point. Then try to view the structure through theodolite and take the collimationaxis to the point whose angle is to be measured. Then click on "Take Reading" to get reading.
